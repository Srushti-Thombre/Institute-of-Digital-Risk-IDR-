About the Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for the evolving challenges of digital, cyber, and technology risk.

By combining academic insight with industry collaboration, IDR bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world implementation. The institute equips students and professionals with the practical skills required to operate in regulated, high-consequence environments such as financial services, critical infrastructure, and emerging technology sectors.

Our Model

Training talent, advancing research, and supporting organizations in navigating digital risk.

Academy

Training programs and intensive bootcamps designed to prepare students and professionals for careers in digital, cyber, and technology risk management.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new intellectual property, governance models, and research initiatives focused on emerging areas such as AI risk, digital resilience, and future regulatory frameworks.

Advisory Services

Helping organizations implement global risk management standards such as NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2 through practical frameworks and industry expertise.

From Learning to Impact

  1. Train

    Developing expertise through structured learning and hands-on training.

  2. Innovate

    Advancing research and developing new risk management approaches.

  3. Hire

    Connecting trained professionals with organizations that require digital risk talent.

  4. Deploy

    Supporting organizations in implementing practical risk frameworks.

Our Community

IDR supports a growing community of students, early-career professionals, and experienced practitioners who want to deepen their expertise in digital and cyber risk. The institute collaborates with professionals across sectors such as financial services, government, technology companies, and critical infrastructure to strengthen resilience in the digital age.
